Welcome to Glimmpse:

The general linear multivariate model power and sample size estimator

This tool is designed to aid researchers in selecting appropriate power and sample size for studies using the general linear multivariate model for data analysis. Common examples of this model include the one and two sample t-test, ANOVA, ANCOVA, MANOVA, and regression.

To lean more, please see the documentation or related publications page. Otherwise, click the start button to begin.

This project was developed at the University of Colorado, department of Biostatistics and Bioinformations. The power calculations are based on the work of Keith Muller at the University of Florida.
